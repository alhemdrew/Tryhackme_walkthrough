## Day 9 - Passwords: A Cracking Christmas

**Room Completed:** ✅

**Tasks and Answers:**

- **Flag inside the encrypted PDF:**  
`THM{Cr4ck1ng_PDFs_1s_34$y}`

- **Flag inside the encrypted ZIP file:**  
`THM{Cr4ck1n6_z1p$_1s_34$yyyy}`

- **Notes:**  
  - Dictionary attacks are often the first choice using wordlists like `rockyou.txt`.  
  - Mask attacks and brute-force can follow if dictionary attacks fail.  
  - Use tools like `pdfcrack`, `fcrackzip`, `john` (`pdf2john`, `zip2john`), and `hashcat`.  
  - Monitor GPU/CPU usage, process creation, and unusual file reads for detection.  
  - Maintain SOC playbooks for isolating hosts and preserving evidence.
